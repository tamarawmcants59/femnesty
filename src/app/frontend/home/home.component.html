Femnesty connects women from all walks of life and all around the world.

What is
Femnesty?

Femnesty is a network that connects women from all around the world. It exists to help women recognise and unleash the potential in their own personal and professional lives, and to promote the positive roles we play as parents, professionals, family members and friends.

Articles

Read inspirational stories from women about the challenges of working, raising families and balancing personal fulfilment with professional success.

Safe Place

Femnesty provides a dedicated Space where you can talk to, learn from and debate with women around the world on the issues that matter to you.

Mentorship

Our mentoring scheme enables women to find, work with, learn from and even become the best mentors in their industry.
