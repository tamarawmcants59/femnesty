Femnesty Hubs

Femnesty Hubs are at the very heart of what we do. These small peer groups meet regularly to discuss issues, share ideas, support one another and encourage professional development. Hubs grow into strong support networks, and these empower women to tackle new challenges and embrace opportunities. Partner with Femnesty and we’ll help you set up Hubs in your workplace. Our online tool will help you establish and manage your network, and we can also provide ideas and materials to help you succeed.

Femnesty Mentoring

Women often make the best mentors for other women because they’ve faced and overcome the same unique challenges themselves. Femnesty can help you establish mentorships within your workplace, or put your female staff in touch with an outside mentor with suitable expertise. Women thrive under mentorships; they feel supported and empowered, and are generally happier in their careers. Companies who offer or support mentorships are therefore more likely to retain female talent, and reduce the gender gap at the highest level. Partner with Femnesty and we can help you set up and maintain a mentorship programme that will benefit not just the women in your business, but your entire organisation.

Corporate Partnerships & Mentoring

Corporate mentoring isn’t about 1-2-1 meetings between well-meaning managers and the people in their teams. Nor is it about setting Key Personal Indictors, discussing targets and agreeing goals. To run an effective Corporate Mentoring programme it’s critical to use qualified Mentors with skills and training in areas such as business confidence, resilience and strategy planning. If these Mentors already work in your organisation, they’re even better placed to guide mentees on their journey to success…

All too often, businesses task managers to mentor the people in their teams. But managers are often not qualified to provide this uniquely specialised support. A quick course or online tutorial is simply not enough to equip a person to take on such an important, empowering role.

That’s why Femnesty has teamed up with an accredited IOEE Academy, Leapfrog Mountain. They provide a range of mentoring and training services that enable businesses to set up or enhance a mentoring programme by supporting the programme design and development and training their own staff to be fully-qualified Mentors and mentor champions.

The qualifications Leapfrog delivers are industry recognised with additional modules of CPD (continuous professional development), so new and existing Mentors can develop their skills. The training is formal, IOEE accredited and quality assured so Mentors – as well as Mentees and the business itself – receive real, long-term benefits for future opportunities and professional success.

As a Femnesty Partner you have full access to Leapfrog’s training and services, which can be delivered online or in person to your specific requirements.

You can also access our directory of trained, professional Mentors should you wish to find another, third-party Mentor to work with you
